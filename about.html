About us

The Text Analytics Research group is a community of researchers who have collective interest of working on different research problems in Data and Text Analytics.

Our main areas of interests are mainly as follows:
  • Scholarly Article Text and Metadata Processing (This includes extracting both bibliometric and textual data from research articles and computationally analysing them. See the publications page for research output in the area)
  • Social Media Data Analytics (This includes tasks like sentiment analysis of social media texts- both at document and aspect levels, sociological and psychological analysis of social media data etc.)
  • Text Document Processing (More fundamental work on Problems like Document Classification, Clustering, Information Extraction etc.)
